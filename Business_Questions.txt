1. Which factors appear to influence subscription renewal the most?
The most influential factors are typically WatchHoursPerWeek, SubscriptionType, MonthlySpend, DevicesUsed, and AdClicks, as these reflect user engagement and subscription value.
2. Why is subscription renewal a classification problem?
Because the target variable has two categories (Yes or No). The model predicts which class a user belongs to.
3. Why is monthly spending a regression problem?
Because monthly spending is a continuous numerical value (amount of money), and the model predicts a number rather than a category.
